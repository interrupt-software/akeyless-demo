Approach Benefits
Establish standardization: Standardize secrets orchestration to resolve the practices differences and unite teams with a shared solution. Teams are neither ahead nor behind in technology choices. An intentional synchronization saves time with testing cycles and minimizes edge cases that may undermine the overall security posture for an organization.
Process alignment: Establish the same process to develop, secure, validate and implement secrets orchestration across all Cloud platforms, VMware and Kubernetes environments. Teams meet the same standards for secrets creation in development, release and maintenance stages. Teams follow guidelines for human interaction, data format, network protocols, ports and secure logical paths for communication.
Strong Governance: With corporate guidance, teams can automate the promotion of secrets orchestration to support emerging standards. Teams can plan for emerging improvements to secrets orchestration and promote new rules using modern DevOps automation patterns from development, to pre-production and to production stages.
