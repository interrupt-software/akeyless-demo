Challenges Consequences
Inconsistency: Different teams have different opinions and practices to deal with secrets materials. When teams have different practices, they are prone to achieve different outcomes with different resolutions to meet governance standards and industry regulations.
Platform diversity: Application workflows vary depending on the target Cloud platforms, VMware or Kubernetes environments. Multiple application workflow processes across multiple target environments introduce significant overhead handling secrets in development, release and maintenance.
Secrets sprawl: Platform diversity across multiple platforms leads to an extensive collection of secrets. Teams that do not track their secrets centrally contribute to inconsistency, operational vulnerabilities and security exposures.
